Ramen

Description

Ramen is a Japanese noodle soup dish that has gained popularity worldwide for its unique and flavorful taste. It typically consists of Chinese-style wheat noodles served in a savory broth, topped with various ingredients such as sliced pork, seaweed, soft-boiled egg, and green onions.

Ingredients

  • 4 cups chicken broth
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon ginger, grated
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 2 teaspoons miso paste
  • 2 packages of ramen noodles
  • 4 soft boiled eggs
  • 4 green onions, sliced
  • 1 cup bean sprouts
  • 1/2 cup sliced mushrooms
  • 1/2 cup shredded carrots
  • 1/4 cup chopped cilantro
  • 1/4 cup sliced jalapeños (optional)

Recipe

  1. In a large pot, bring the chicken broth, garlic, ginger, soy sauce, and miso paste to a boil. Reduce the heat and simmer for 10 minutes.
  2. Cook the ramen noodles according to the package instructions. Drain and rinse with cold water.
  3. Add the cooked ramen noodles to the pot of broth and stir well.
  4. Divide the ramen and broth into four bowls. Top each bowl with a soft boiled egg, sliced green onions, bean sprouts, sliced mushrooms, shredded carrots, cilantro, and sliced jalapeños (if using).
  5. Serve immediately.
